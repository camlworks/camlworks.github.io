Caml Works

Building an OCaml ecosystem that is a pleasure to use.

Caml Works develops the OCaml ecosystem — neat, easy-to-use libraries, excellent documentation, language onboarding process, and the like.

Caml Works was formed by a group of experienced ecosystem contributors, as a kind of R&D cooperative. All of our output continues to be open-sourced under free licenses and donated to the community.

We are initially focusing on developing the Web framework Dream, contributing to related libraries, and smoothing out any friction in OCaml programming along the way.

Who are are

Anton Bachin

  • Author of Dream, the Web framework, including the API reference, online playground, and thorough examples.
  • Maintainer of Lwt, the promise and concurrent I/O library, during 2016-2020.
  • Maintainer of Bisect_ppx, the OCaml coverage tool, since 2016.
  • Author of Lambda Soup and Markup.ml, the two layers of OCaml's spec-compliant HTML5 parser.
  • Author of Luv, an easy-to-use binding to libuv, the C library at the core of Node.js, and thus effectively a full portable systems I/O library.
  • Maintainer of odoc during 2017-2020, and author of its current docstring parser.
  • Author of Promise, a type-safe binding to JavaScript promises.
  • Contributor to discuss.ocaml.org.
  • Experienced developer in other languages, for example C++, familiar with programming on Linux, macOS, Windows, and FreeBSD.

Antonio Nuno Monteiro

  • Maintainer of Melange, a fork of ReScript oriented towards the OCaml ecosystem.
  • Author of h2, OCaml's implementation of HTTP/2.
  • Author of ocaml-quic, an implementation of HTTP/3.
  • Maintainer of advanced forks of http/af and websocketaf, implementing HTTP/1.1 and WebSockets, respectively.
  • Author of piaf, an HTTP client library.
  • Maintainer of nix-overlays, a repository for reproducible OCaml builds with Nix.

Ulrik Strid

  • Author of ocaml-oidc, the OCaml social login library certified by OpenID Connect.
  • Author of reason-jose, an OCaml implementation of JWT and related JSON-based Web security standards.
  • Author of Morph, a Web framework which helped inspire Dream.
  • Author of the Luv port of Lwt, a major reconciliation between OCaml system I/O libraries.
  • Experienced with Docker, Kubernetes, and CI pipelines.
